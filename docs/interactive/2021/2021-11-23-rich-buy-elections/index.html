Note: The plot illustrates the relationship between fair access to state funding of parties and campaign donations (higher values - fairer access), and the capacity of the rich to buy elections (higher values - rich more influential) by the overall level of electoral integrity. It shows that a fairer access to state funding and private contributions is associated with a higher level of electoral integrity and lower ability of the rich to buy elections. Data reflects expert opinions. Use the mouse to rotate/spin, zoom in, and hover over the plot for more details. Data source: (Garnett et al., 2022)

References
